Our mission is to empower traditionally underserved communities by delivering quality computer language education to the youth. Our volunteer instructors are dedicated and experienced experts whose career portfolios include leading institutions such as NASA, Facebook, and Google.

Our new community center is undergoing a construction! Here, children are provided computers and safespace to learn new computer languages. Our goal is to familiarize the youth with the computer coding process so that learning becomes second nature back at home.

We are currently looking for new opportunities. If you are interested in contributing to our awesome cause as a volunteer, endower, or a sister organization, please free to contact us at lets.talk@-----.org or via the Contact Us button located on top of this screen.
