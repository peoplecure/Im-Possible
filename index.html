Our mission is to empower traditionally underserved communities by delivering quality computer language education to the youth. Our volunteer instructors are dedicated and experienced experts whose career portfolios include leading institutions such as NASA, Facebook, and Google.

Our organization has a solid foundation. Currently, we are building a community center to serve as our teaching facility. Here, children will be provided with computers and a safespace to learn cool computer languages. Our goal is to familiarize the youth with the computer programming process so that learning becomes second nature at home.

We are looking for partnership opportunities and likeminded individuals to make a positive impact. If you are interested in contributing to our awesome cause as a volunteer, endower, or a sister organization, feel free to reach us at lets.talk@-----.org or via Contact Us button located on top of this page.
