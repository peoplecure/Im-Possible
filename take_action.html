We are Narrowing the Education Gap



When we are teaching children how to code, we are making an impact by narrowing the education gap in communities where children reside. Teaching computer programming is our way of mentoring and coaching children toward their success, and every child is supported by a network of parents, facilitators, program coordinators, and community members who share the same value of developing a strong community. But, we need your support to keep this project going. Although I’m Possible project has devoted partners in some areas of D.C., we need to connect with more likeminded individuals to spread the cause.
